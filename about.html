About

Personal

I'm based in Chicago, where I split my time between designing, running the lakefront, and chasing light with a compact film camera. Offline, I’m usually cooking something in a cast iron skillet or reading about how people work — and how we might work better. I like things that are well made, quietly clever, and just a little unexpected.

Design Philosophy

I believe the best design is invisible: grounded in structure, built to scale, and shaped by clarity. I gravitate toward systems — type ramps, grid logic, reusable patterns — not for their own sake, but because they free up space for ideas to shine. Whether I’m working on enterprise tools or consumer-facing products, I aim for design that’s visually refined, purposeful, and built to last.

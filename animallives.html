Yale / NASA — Product Design — 2023

I worked with scientists from Yale and NASA to design an educational tool that brings scientific data on animal life spans into public conversation. Our goal was to translate raw data into an interactive experience that’s accessible, engaging, and grounded in ecological impact.

I led the design of the site’s interface, ensuring clarity across devices and creating systems that scaled with new content. The final product was a clean, responsive site used by teachers, researchers, and casual learners alike.

Date: Jan–May 2023
Project Type: Product Design
Client: Yale & NASA
